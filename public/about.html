Nick Lambrou is a filmmaker born and raised in Athens, Greece, now based in Los Angeles, California. Drawn to filmmaking from an early age, he began creating YouTube videos and conceptual photoshoots. He earned a B.A. in Media Art Production from Emerson College in Boston, Massachusetts and gained industry experience through internships at BBDO, FilmNation Entertainment, AfterBuzz TV, and Feelgood Entertainment. With a global perspective and a deep understanding of international audiences, Nick develops, writes, and produces projects for TV and Feature Films, crafting stories that transcend cultural boundaries.
