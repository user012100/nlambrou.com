I’m Nick, a 23 year old filmmaker currently based in Los Angeles, California. I’ve always been attracted to the beauty of filmmaking and the depths and mysteries of the artistic world. I got my first camera and started taking pictures of my friends at the age of 11. I have always been inspired by human beauty and my Greek heritage. My style usually incorporates the aesthetics influenced by the streets of Athens, while still maintaining an American retro flair. I’m always working on a project whether that’s album cover photoshoots or a short film; always trying to incorporate art in my daily life.
